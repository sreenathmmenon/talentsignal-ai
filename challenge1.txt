TRACK 1 — HACKATHON
For: Developers, AI/ML Engineers, Data Scientists

The challenge: Build an intelligent AI-powered candidate ranking system that goes beyond keyword filters and actually surfaces the right people for a role.

-----
Develop a robust, workable Proof of Concept that doesn't just filter, but intelligently ranks candidates.

Your system should act as the ultimate AI recruiter, capable of:

Deep Job Understanding: Interpreting complex, nuanced job descriptions.

Contextual Relevance: Seeing beyond keywords to understand semantic fit.

Signal Integration: Leveraging all data: profile attributes, career metadata, and crucial activity/behavioral signals.

The Output: Delivering a lightning-fast, highly accurate, and expertly ranked shortlist of the best-fit candidates.

No fixed architecture→unleash your innovation→Bring the best of your creativity.
-----



📦You will receive:
A large candidate dataset
A job description
Complete freedom over architecture — any stack, any approach
⚖️You will be judged on:
Quality of your ranking output
Clarity of your methodology and reasoning
Whether your system is explainable
📤What to submit:
A working GitHub repository with your complete code
A ranked candidate output file in the provided format
A methodology document explaining your approach and technical choices
