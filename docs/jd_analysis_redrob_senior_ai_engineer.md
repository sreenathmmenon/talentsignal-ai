# JD Analysis: Redrob Senior AI Engineer

## Role Summary

Redrob needs a founding-team Senior AI Engineer to own the intelligence layer of a talent intelligence product. The core work is ranking, retrieval, matching, evaluation, and product-facing ML systems.

## What The JD Really Means

This is a product ML systems role, not a research role and not a prompt-engineering demo role. The strongest candidate has built user-facing search/ranking/retrieval/recommendation systems and can ship useful ranking improvements quickly.

## Scorecard Dimensions

### Core Technical Fit

- Production embeddings/retrieval/ranking/search/recommendation.
- Vector or hybrid search infrastructure.
- Python and ML systems engineering.
- Ranking/retrieval evaluation.
- LLM/fine-tuning experience where backed by production judgment.

### Product And Company Fit

- Product-company, startup, marketplace, HR-tech, search, recommendation, or user-facing ML background.
- Evidence of shipping practical systems.
- Ownership mindset and ability to work with PM/recruiter workflows.

### Seniority Fit

- Preferred: 5-9 years.
- Strongest expected range: 6-8 years with 4-5 years applied AI/ML.
- Outliers allowed when technical evidence is strong.

### Location And Logistics

- Strong: Pune, Noida, Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Bangalore.
- Good: India with relocation willingness.
- Weaker: outside India without relocation/work authorization fit.
- Notice period below 30 days is best; longer notice raises the bar.

### Behavioral Hireability

- Recently active.
- Open to work.
- Responsive to recruiters.
- Verified email/phone/LinkedIn.
- Good interview completion and offer acceptance.
- Recruiter saves/search appearances support market interest.

## Strong Positive Evidence

- Career history explicitly mentions production ranking, retrieval, search, recommender, embeddings, vector database, hybrid search, BM25, FAISS, Qdrant, Milvus, Pinecone, Weaviate, Elasticsearch, OpenSearch.
- Career history mentions online/offline evaluation, NDCG, MRR, MAP, A/B testing, recruiter feedback loops.
- Current or recent title includes ML Engineer, AI Engineer, Applied ML Engineer, Search Engineer, Recommendation Systems Engineer, Senior Software Engineer (ML), Data Scientist with product ML evidence, Backend/Data Engineer with search/retrieval evidence.

## Strong Negative Evidence

- Current title and career are unrelated to AI/ML engineering.
- AI terms appear only in skills, not career history.
- Summary says "curious about AI" or "experimented with ChatGPT" without production experience.
- Pure academic/research background with no deployed systems.
- Skill stuffing: many expert AI skills with no duration/endorsement/assessment support.
- Stale/low-response candidate despite strong static profile.

## Ranking Policy

- Top 10 must have direct production ML/search/retrieval/ranking evidence.
- Top 50 may include adjacent engineers with strong evidence.
- Top 100 may include plausible adjacent candidates but should avoid obvious traps.
- Career evidence outranks skill-list evidence.
- Behavioral signals should modify final hireability.
