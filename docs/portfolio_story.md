# Portfolio Story

## Short Pitch

TalentSignal AI is an agentic hiring-intelligence project that turns a job description into a scorecard, mines candidate evidence, ranks candidates with transparent factor scores, flags risk, and generates recruiter-ready shortlists.

## Technical Summary

The system is a deterministic Python ranker built for a 100,000-candidate challenge dataset. It uses configurable job specs, source-separated feature extraction, local talent graph dictionaries, risk audit rules, factor scoring, grounded explanations, validation scripts, and a static recruiter cockpit demo.

## Recruiter-Friendly Summary

TalentSignal AI helps recruiters move from keyword search to evidence-backed shortlists. It explains why each candidate is ranked, highlights risks, and keeps the human recruiter in control.

## LinkedIn/GitHub Wording

Built TalentSignal AI, an evidence-based candidate ranking system for a Redrob AI hiring challenge. The project processes 100,000 candidate profiles locally, ranks the top 100 for a Senior AI Engineer JD, produces grounded explanations, exports factor scores and risk audits, and includes a recruiter cockpit demo.

## Claims To Avoid

- Do not claim legal compliance.
- Do not claim bias elimination.
- Do not claim access to hidden labels.
- Do not claim final hiring decisions are automated.
